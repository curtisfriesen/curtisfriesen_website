Co-owers and operators: Vincent Sica & Robert Colavecchia along with their talented exceptional staff are dedicated to transforming your wedding dreams into reality.

Centrally located in the Niagara Region, our beautifully appointed & recently renovated facility can accommodate from 100 - 400 guests.

Offering artisanal cuisine combined with impeccable service and attention to detail in warm and inviting setting. Our professional culinary team offers a variety of inspired classic and contemporary preparations to satisfy all tastes and budgets.
