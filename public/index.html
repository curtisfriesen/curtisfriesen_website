About

In 2017 I graduated from the Computer Programmer Analyst (Co-op) program at Niagara College. Throughout my schooling I built of variety of web, mobile, and windows based applications, as well as developed my communcation skills and computer hardware knowledge.

I currently work at iPipeline Canada as a Software Developer. We are a very diverse team focused on building advanced web applications for the life insurance world. Previous positions include working at ServicePRO as a UI/UX Designer and Developer, using Material Design to design a captivating user interface. I also worked as a Computer Research Associate at Niagara College Research & Innovation where we used Angular to build a web application for farmers across Canada to analyze and track their crop growth.

Outside of my personal life I enjoy spending my time playing a variety of video games and spending time with my beautiful fiance, friends and family.
